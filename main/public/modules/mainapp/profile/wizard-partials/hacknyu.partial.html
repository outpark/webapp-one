Register for HACKNYU 2016

Hackathons are invention competitions — Designers, programmers, builders and experts in various fields come together to learn, build, and share their creations over the course of a few days. Hackathons are not limited to computer science majors — anyone who has an interest in technology and is eager to learn can participate in a hackathon.
HACKNYU is a collaborative challenge to create transformative technologies that challenge education, healthcare and dentistry, environmental sustainability, accessiblity issues and current financial systems. Students from accross the nation and globally in Abu Dhabi and Shanghai are tackling focused problems that help millions of people around the globe. HACKNYU fosters a new and refreshing culture of creation, invention and innovation through partnerships between makers, industry leaders, and community members.
